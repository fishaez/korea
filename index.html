Old capital, then the fast train north

⚑ Longest day of the trip — see note below

Check out with bags, day-trip through Gyeongju, then continue straight to Seoul by KTX instead of backtracking to Busan.

  1. 07:30

    Breakfast, check out

    Bags come with you — no return to Busan tonight.

  2. 08:15

    KTX Busan → Singyeongju

    ~20–30 min.

  3. 09:00

    Arrive Gyeongju, taxi to Bulguksa

  4. 09:30–11:00

    Bulguksa Temple

    UNESCO-listed, Korea's best-known Buddhist temple.

  5. 11:00–12:00

    Shuttle up, short uphill walk at the top.

⚑ Two things to check first

  • Chuseok 2026 falls close to this window — confirm the exact dates, since it affects KTX availability and whether some sites/restaurants close for the holiday.
  • Day 3 is long — Gyeongju plus a late KTX into Seoul. Cutting Seokguram Grotto is the easiest way to shorten it if the group needs it.

Trains to book early

  • ICN → Gimhae, domestic flight (Day 1)
  • Busan → Singyeongju, KTX (Day 3 morning)
  • Singyeongju → Seoul, KTX (Day 3 night)

Book via Korail or the Klook/Trainpal apps — reserve all 4 seats together, especially this close to Chuseok.
