⚑ Two things to check first

  • Chuseok 2026 falls close to this window — confirm the exact dates, since it affects KTX availability and whether some sites/restaurants close for the holiday.
  • Day 3 is long — Gyeongju plus a late KTX into Seoul. Cutting Seokguram Grotto is the easiest way to shorten it if the group needs it.

Trains to book early

  • ICN → Gimhae, domestic flight (Day 1, all 4 together)
  • Busan → Singyeongju, KTX (Day 3 morning)
  • Singyeongju → Seoul, KTX (Day 3 night)

Book via Korail or the Klook/Trainpal apps — this close to Chuseok, don't wait.
